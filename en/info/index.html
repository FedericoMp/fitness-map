Info

Run into your unknown

The web app was built to share differents ways to do physical activities, there are a lot of options to choose. The map and the main points are based on my personal and favorites activities. You can walk, hiking or running in woods or beaches, training calisthenics and even skating or ride a bike.

Don't wait to act

In spite of the weather conditions you can go and make your personal trip a unique adventure.

And why not combine more than one, I think doing physical activities is great for wellbeing, keep healthy and active.

Take your action today

Every activity counts, there are a lot of beautiful nature locationes to discover, go for a walk, see the sunset, make friends or training.

Very intuitive and super easy to use, just go to the map tab, select a place to go and enjoy.
